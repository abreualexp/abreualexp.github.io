Alex Abreu is a master's student at the Department of Production Engineering of the Federal University of São Carlos (UFSCar), Brazil, under the supervision of Prof. Pedro Munari. He received a bachelor's degree in Production Engineering from the Federal University of Goiás (UFG), Brazil. Alex has worked on logistics and production optimization including the pickup and delivery problem and the flow shop scheduling problem.

Research Interests:

  • Operations Research
  • Mathematical modelling
  • Vehicle routing problems
  • Scheduling problems
  • Robust optimization
